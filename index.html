Afghan Turk Cag Schools




Afghan Turk High Schools, operated by the Afghan-Turk Cag Educational NGO,
play a significant role in the educational landscape of Afghanistan. Founded on principles
rooted in the Gulen movement, these high schools aim to provide a holistic education that goes
beyond academic excellence, emphasizing character development,
inter-cultural understanding, and global citizenship.

Educational philosophy of Afghan Turk High Schools is centered around creating well-rounded individuals equipped with not only academic knowledge but also ethical values. The curriculum is designed to encourage critical thinking, creativity, and a sense of social responsibility. Emphasizing dialogue and understanding, the schools aim to prepare students for active and positive participation in their communities and the wider world.

powerful tool for transforming individuals
and societies. He believes that an enlightened and educated society
is essential for peace, stability, and progress.
Gulen emphasizes the role of education in instilling moral
and ethical values in individuals. He believes that a holistic education
should not only focus on academic knowledge but also on character development and the cultivation
of virtues such as compassion, empathy, and justice.
